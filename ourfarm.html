Our Farm & Sustainability

At Alawusa Heritage Fish & Farms

At Alawusa Heritage Fish & Farms, our farming isn't just a business — it’s a family tradition, built on over 40 years of hands-on agricultural experience and a deep commitment to sustainable food production in Nigeria. From our ponds to our poultry pens, everything we do is guided by our values: Heritage, Quality, Freshness, Sustainability, Trust, Community, and Food Security.



Fish Farming: Clean, Controlled, and Community-Focused


We raise high-quality Catfish and other fish using well-managed, hygienic pond systems. Our fish are fed with protein-rich, nutrient-balanced feed —
and our water management practices ensure freshness, safety, and rapid growth.



Our Practices:


  • Regular water monitoring and oxygen control

  • Natural probiotics to reduce antibiotic use

  • Safe harvesting and unique smoking techniques for signature smoked fish

  • Local job creation through community-based processing and retail

    • “Our fish are clean, delicious, and proudly Nigerian.”



    Poultry Farming: Healthy Chickens, Fresh Eggs


    Our poultry operation includes layers and broilers, producing fresh eggs and chicken meat with care.



    Our Practices:


  • Spacious, ventilated deep-litter pens

  • Organic feed supplements and clean water

  • 14+ hours of lighting to support natural laying cycles

  • Strict biosecurity and vaccination protocols

    • “Every egg we produce is a promise of nutrition, freshness, and quality.”



    Pig Farming: Smart Waste Use, Safe Production


    Pigs are raised in purpose-built pens that ensure hygiene and animal welfare. Waste is never wasted — it’s recycled into compost or even biogas where possible.



    Our Practices


  • Sloped concrete pens with proper drainage

  • Daily cleaning and use of Effective Microorganisms (EM) for odor control

  • Composting manure into organic fertilizer

  • Long-term goal: Biogas system to turn waste into energy

    • “We turn pig waste into power, not pollution.”



    Our Sustainability Commitment


    Sustainability isn’t a buzzword — it’s our way of life. We are committed to:



  • Integrated Farming: Reducing waste by reusing resources across fish, poultry, and pigs

  • Eco-Friendly Practices: Composting, water recycling, and local feed sourcing

  • Community Empowerment: Hiring and training local youth and women

  • Food Security: Providing fresh, affordable protein for Nigerian homes

  • Zero Waste Goal: Every part of the farm serves a purpose — nothing is left behind

    • “We farm today with tomorrow in mind.”



    Join Us on the Journey

    you’re backing food security, sustainability, and community growth in Nigeria.
